### Introduction

The Guardian friend passionately advocates for social change and justice, leading protests and encouraging others to join their cause. From standing up to your bullies to educating you about social issues, they are the friend who fights for you and gets you fighting. *However, they often wrestle with feelings of burnout and frustration when change seems slow or non-existent, and fear their efforts may not be enough.*
